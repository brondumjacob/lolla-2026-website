About Lolla Lineup 2026

An unofficial fan-made lineup guide

What Is This?

This site provides the complete Lollapalooza 2026 lineup with one-click links to every artist on Spotify and Apple Music. It was built to make it easy to discover and preview all 172 artists performing at Grant Park this summer.

Features

Search any artist by name, filter by genre (Pop, Rock, Hip-Hop, Electronic, EDM, Indie, K-Pop, R&B, and more), or browse by tier (Headliners, Major Acts, Undercard). Every artist links directly to their streaming page so you can start listening immediately. Filter state is shareable via URL.

The lineup information is based on the official Lollapalooza 2026 announcement from March 2026. Daily schedules and stage assignments have not yet been announced and are not included. If you notice any errors or missing artists, feel free to reach out.
